Side Hustle week 3 Task

Salary Calculator for Side Hustle workers

instructions for using the calculator:

The function name is 'salary', and it accepts two argument if the employee has extra hour

The function also works with one argument in case the employee doesn't work any extra hour

The value of hours entered (in the argument) is multiplied by 1000 and the value of extra hour worked is also multiplied by 200 then the sum of the two multiplications is then returned as the total salary to be paid by the company to the employee
